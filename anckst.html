ANCKST

branding project

ANCKST is a fictitious brand for anxiety jewelry that was created as a group project in another corporate design course. Our goal was to create a self-confident and loud brand that aims to raise awareness for anxiety and mental health in general. The name ANCKST is an allusion to the German word "Angst", which translates to "fear" or "anxiety". We were inspired by brutalist design without losing sensitivity in relation to the topic. It is this balance of brutalism and sensuality that is visible in the heart of our design - the imagery. All four of us were involved in every aspect of this project, but I was personally most involved in the design and implementation of the website prototype.
